Meet The Artist

Ever since I've picked up a pencil, I've always been drawing on anything I could get my hands on. Such as napkins, newspapers, and even toilet paper. I would always be found in the living room at home sitting on the floor and staring at the cartoons before me. I realized along the years that I wanted to give people the exact same joy I had felt watching animated films.

Five Main Goals....

Future Plans For Employment

Animator 

Main Goal

I mainly wish to go for this job because I find it fascinating how much work is put into each step of the process of bringing art to life, and being able to create a story that way.

Comic Book Artist

Second Most Important Goal

My main inspiration along the years when it comes to art is consisting of reading comics whenever I could get the chance. It amazed me how comics would be made with a set of people at a time, and how much recognition some characters in an industry like that were able to be represented in the media. 

Video Game Artist

Third Most Important Goal

Video games nowadays whether they're 2D or 3D are worked on for about a year and have multiple sets of teams all focused on one main goal. I would like to be a part of a company like this in a way that when it comes to designing characters and landscapes and how the overall game looks in terms of design.

Storyboard Artist

While storyboarding isn't as expressive as the actual animation itself,
I feel that drawing different panels can show just as much life as it can with ever single
movement animated. 

Illustrator

As much as I love to tell stories through art, I
wouldn't mind recreating somebody else's with art.
